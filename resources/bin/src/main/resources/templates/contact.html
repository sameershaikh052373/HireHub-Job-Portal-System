Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar and follow the steps. You can choose to register as a job seeker or an employer depending on your needs.

Yes, currently we offer free job posting for all employers. However, we also offer premium features and promoted job listings for a fee.

To apply for a job, browse our job listings and click on any job that interests you. On the job details page, you'll find an "Apply Now" button. Click it and follow the instructions to submit your application, including your cover letter and resume.

After logging into your account, navigate to the "My Applications" section from the dashboard. There you can view the status of all your job applications and any updates from employers.
